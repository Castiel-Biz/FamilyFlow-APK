**FamilyFlow Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date: April 2026**

FamilyFlow is a private family organizer app designed to help families stay organized with shared calendars, tasks, grocery lists, group chat, finance tracking, notes, and a helpful AI assistant — while keeping your data simple, local, and secure.

### 1. Information We Collect
FamilyFlow is built to be **offline-first**. Most of your data (calendar events, tasks, grocery lists, chat messages, notes, and financial entries) is stored **locally on your device** and is never sent to our servers.

We only collect or process:
- Voice input when you use the AI assistant (processed on your device or sent to the AI provider you choose, such as Grok/xAI, OpenAI, or Claude, using the API key you provide).
- Anonymous crash and usage data to help fix bugs.
- Optional family sync data (only the information you choose to share with your family group).

We do **not** collect:
- Location data
- Contacts (except for family invite codes)
- Advertising identifiers
- Personal data for marketing or selling

### 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide the core features of the app.
- To improve the app through anonymous crash reports.
- To enable optional family synchronization between invited family members.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

- When you use the AI assistant and provide your own API key, your input goes directly to that provider (Grok/xAI, OpenAI, Claude, etc.). We do not store or access those conversations.
- Shared family data is only visible to the family members you invite.
- We may disclose information if required by law.

### 4. Data Storage and Security
- Most data remains stored locally on your device.
- We use industry-standard security practices for any synced data.
- You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

### 5. Children's Privacy
FamilyFlow is intended for family use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

### 6. Your Rights
You can access, correct, or delete your local data inside the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

### 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

### 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the in-app feedback form or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
